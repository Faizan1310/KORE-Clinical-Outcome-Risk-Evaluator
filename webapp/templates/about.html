KORE is an end-to-end AI-powered clinical decision support system designed to predict hospital readmission risk and empower healthcare professionals.

üè• The Problem

Hospital readmissions are one of the most costly and preventable challenges in healthcare. When a patient is discharged and returns within 30 days, it signals incomplete treatment, clinical instability, or inadequate follow-up care.

üí° Hospital readmissions cost the US healthcare system $26 billion annually. Approximately 20% of patients are readmitted within 30 days ‚Äî and up to 70% of those readmissions are preventable with the right interventions.

The core problem: hospitals have no reliable, data-driven tool to identify high-risk patients before they leave. Doctors rely on intuition and experience ‚Äî but no single physician can learn from 101,766 patient cases simultaneously. KORE can.

KORE combines traditional machine learning with modern Generative AI to provide complete clinical decision support ‚Äî not just a number, but actionable intelligence.

üî¨ How It Was Built

Data Collection & Exploration

Used the Diabetes 130-US Hospitals Dataset ‚Äî 101,766 real patient records from 130 hospitals collected over 10 years (1999-2008). Analyzed 50 clinical features including demographics, diagnoses, medications, lab procedures, and outcomes.

Data Preprocessing

Cleaned missing values, removed irrelevant features, applied Label Encoding to convert categorical data to numerical format. Created binary target variable: HIGH RISK (readmitted within 30 days) vs LOW RISK.

Handling Class Imbalance with SMOTE

The dataset had 90,409 LOW RISK vs 11,357 HIGH RISK patients ‚Äî an 8:1 imbalance. Applied SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Oversampling Technique) to create synthetic HIGH RISK samples and balance both classes.

Model Training

Trained a Random Forest Classifier with 50 decision trees on the balanced dataset. Achieved 91% accuracy and 0.957 ROC-AUC on the held-out test set ‚Äî well above the 0.80 industry benchmark for medical ML models.

AI Integration

Integrated Groq API with Llama 3.3 70B to generate AI Doctor Reports, AI Recommendations, and a multilingual chatbot supporting 7 languages ‚Äî transforming raw predictions into actionable clinical insights.

Deployment

Built Flask web application with SQLite database for prediction history. Deployed live on Render.com using Gunicorn as the production WSGI server. Secured API keys via environment variables.
